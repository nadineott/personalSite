BarHawk
Often times drunk driving incidents occur because people simply don't know how incapacitated they are. BarHawk is a drink ordering app that tracks a users alcohol consumption and uses previously stored information (age, weight, etc) to calculates blood alcohol content in real time to help responsible adults make educated decisions.

FunFinder
FunFinder was created to solve the "tabs to infinity" problem when searching for a fun activity on a given day. Now, instead of searching Meetup, volunteer sites, and movie theatre listings for activities, one can to to the one stop shop for fun- FunFinder.
MakerPair
MakerSquare (a software engineering immersive in the heart of San Francisco) was faced with a process of determining unque mini-sprint pairs of students on a recurring basis. This time saving app takes the class names and instantly generates the pairs for the entirety of the pair-programming sprints.
